About

Founded in South Africa and based between Amsterdam and Cape Town, creative duo Anna Thomas and Kim Verburg merge their expertise in carpentry and architecture to create furniture that is both functional and playful. Currently, Thomas & Verburg have a strong focus on benches – thoughtfully produced in small batches using locally sourced wood. However, this is just the beginning. Our vision is to grow into a broader design space, exploring new forms, materials, and ideas that push the boundaries of contemporary furniture. Follow along as we evolve and grow!

Order

Our benches are made in small, carefully considered batches using locally harvested wood sourced in South Africa. Crafted by skilled small-scale carpenters, each piece reflects quality, craftsmanship, and sustainability. Since we work with the wood we have on hand, availability may vary. While we strive to keep stock of our collections as displayed online, we cannot always guarantee immediate availability. To check current stock, please email us — we'd be happy to assist! You can also sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know when new stock becomes available. For any other inquiries, feel free to get in touch. We'd love to hear from you.
